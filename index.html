About Us

More than 70 years ago, Hospital Bendaña opened its doors to offer the population one of the most essential services for people: health.
Since then, Hospital Bendaña has strived to remain as one of the main alternatives of health services in the region with the best medical technology at the service of its patients.

Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to Hospital Bendaña's Frequently Asked Questions section.
In this section, we have gathered the most common questions we receive from our patients and their families. Our goal is to provide you with the information you need in a clear and accessible manner, so you can have a more comfortable and hassle-free experience at our hospital.
Whether you need to know about our hours of operation, how to schedule an appointment, the services we offer or any other questions you may have, you'll find quick and helpful answers here. If you don't find the information you're looking for, don't hesitate to contact us directly; we're here to help.

  • What are the hospital's opening hours ?

    Our Hospital is open Monday through Sunday from 8:00 a.m. to 6:00 p.m. and on Saturdays from 9:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.

  • How can I schedule an appointment?

    You can schedule an appointment by calling our phone number (+504 2516-2980), through our website in the appointments section, or by visiting us directly at the Hospital.

  • Do you accept insurance?

    Yes, we accept most major medical insurances. To confirm if we accept your insurance, please see our insurance section or call us directly.

  • What should I bring on my first appointment?

    Please bring your ID, your health insurance card, and any relevant medical records. If you are taking medication, it is helpful to bring a list of your medications.

  • What are your cancellation policies?

    If you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment, we ask that you do so at least 24 hours in advance to avoid additional charges.

  • Do you offer online consultation?

    Sí, ofrecemos consultas en línea para ciertos tipos de consultas. Puedes agendar una consulta en línea a través de nuestro sitio web o llamándonos.
